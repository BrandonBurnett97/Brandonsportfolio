Objective: Create a poster showing the pros of aging.

A company called Ageworks was looking for a poster design to advertise their movement on the positives on aging. They were looking for a poster that tells people that aging isn't so bad, and that there are benefits to being a senior.

Often times we see the granparents babysitting the children while the parents are working. Talking with senoirs, some of them stated that they enjoy spending time with children because it's paying it forward to the next generation. That was the inspiration for this poster.

The watch represents the knowledge and experience of aging with time, paying it forward to the new generation. The message behind this poster is that it's important to remember those who taught us and that one day we will be old, but we will have more knowledge and wisdom to give to the world when we're gone.
